Paa-ion Chonburi

A seafood restaurant in Trok Kuan, Chonburi, famous for its fresh, spicy mantis shrimp, crabs, shrimp, and scallops at reasonable prices (rice dishes start at 100-150 baht). The atmosphere is like sitting at an aunt's house. There are only a few tables, but the food is served quickly and the service is attentive. It's perfect for those who want to eat delicious seafood in the middle of Chonburi, it's best to come early. The shop is open from 7:00 AM to 2:00 PM and only accepts cash.
