Kopihub Chonburi

Kopihub Chonburi is a popular dim sum restaurant known for fresh, delicious food and efficient service. Located conveniently in the heart of Chonburi city, it’s a great spot for breakfast or brunch, especially before heading to Bangsaen beach early in the morning. The restaurant offers beautiful presentation, cozy atmosphere, and reasonable prices. It’s perfect for anyone craving authentic dim sum made fresh daily.
