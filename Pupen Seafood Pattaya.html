Pupen seafood Chonburi

Pupen Seafood is a well-known seafood restaurant located in Chonburi, offering a wide variety of fresh and flavorful seafood dishes. The restaurant is popular among locals and tourists alike for its quality ingredients, generous portions, and authentic Thai seafood flavors. Whether you want to enjoy grilled prawns, steamed fish, or spicy seafood salad, Pupen Seafood delivers tasty meals that satisfy every seafood lover’s cravings. Conveniently situated near the coast and easy to reach from the city center, Pupen Seafood provides a relaxed dining atmosphere with both indoor and outdoor seating options. The prices are reasonable considering the freshness and quality of the seafood served. It’s a perfect place for family gatherings, casual dinners, or a special meal when visiting Chonburi.
